How to Play

  • Place both hands in front of the camera.
  • Closed hands represent the car is in neutral.
  • Pointing up the index finger means 1st gear.
  • Pointing up the index and middle fingers means 2nd gear.
  • Pointing up the index, middle, and ring fingers means 3rd gear.
  • Pointing up the index, middle, ring, and small fingers means 4th gear.
  • Opening all hands means stop and reverse the car.
  • Move your hands like a steering wheel to handle the car.
